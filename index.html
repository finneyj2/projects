Jahlia Finney is a researcher and aspiring data analyst with a background in psychology and computer science. This blog is a place to display her data projects. She will be writing about the obstacles that she is working though that could be useful for other aspiring data analysts. When not programming, she enjoys cooking, watching terrifying movies and playing with cats(not necessarily her own).
