Music Therapy 1

Step 0, April 21, 2024

The dataset that I am using has come entirely from Catharine Rasgaitis’s Music and Mental Health Survey. Despite this, I largely ignore the mental health data that was collected from the survey. I decided to ignore this information due to the dataset’s limitations. The survey asks if the participants’ have OCD, Anxiety, Depression or Insomnia, but does not inquire about diagnosis status. And additionally, the severity of the mental illness experienced by participants is entirely measured by self reports. Generally, this negatively impacts precision and accuracy, so I did not include the mental health data.
