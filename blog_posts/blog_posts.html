Property Value Blog Post 1- Missing Values

The pre-first step step, March 11, 2021

Coming into this analysis of property values, I had three main elements that I wanted to investigate

  1. How much the price of properties in PA changed approximately 8 years.
  2. How much property values changed in PA per city
  3. And the average price of a home per school district
Before I discuss my questions for this dataset and how I approached answering them, I’m going to list it’s basic properties. The Property-Values dataset had 307755 individual entries across 23 columns. The columns included were Property-House-Number, Property-Fraction, Property-Address-Dir, Property-Address-Street, Property-Address-Suf, Property-Address-Unit-Desc, Property-Unit-Number, Property-City, Property-State, Property-Zip, School-Code, School-Desc, Municipal-Code, Municipal-District, Recorded-Date, Sale-Date, price, DeedBook, DeedPage, SaleCode, Sale-Desc, Instrument Type, and Instrument Description.
